Chocolate "I Scream" Cookie Sandwiches

Description:

These ice cream cookie sandwiches are a fun and easy treat for Halloween or any other occasion. You can use store-bought cookies and ice cream, and decorate them with melted chocolate to create spooky or cute designs. The trick is to pipe the chocolate on the cookies while they are still wet, and avoid touching up the mistakes. You can also customize the flavors and colors of the cookies and ice cream to suit your preferences. These ice cream cookie sandwiches are sure to impress your guests with their creative and delicious appearance. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 8 large chocolate chip cookies
  • 3 tablespoons melted dark chocolate
  • 4 teaspoons melted white chocolate
  • Â½ cup vanilla ice cream

Directions:

    Step 1:

  • Gather all ingredients and Cover the flat side of 4 cookies with melted dark chocolate; tap the cookies lightly to smooth out the chocolate.

    • Step 2:

  • While still wet, use a piping bag or spoon to make a ghost shape with the white chocolate; tap lightly once more to smooth as needed. More white designs can be added to the dark chocolate background if desired.

    • Step 3:

  • Use melted dark chocolate to make the eyes and screaming mouth; tap or shake once more to even and smooth out.

    • Step 4:

  • Let chocolate cool until hard and dry to the touch, at least 1 hour.

    • Step 5:

  • Scoop ice cream on 4 remaining cookies, and cover with the tops. Carefully press to flatten, and transfer to a freezer to freeze thoroughly before serving.

    • Step 6:

  • Serve and enjoy!
